# Privacy Policy for Pic2Nav

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

Pic2Nav ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **Precise Location**: We collect GPS coordinates from your device to identify locations, provide nearby places, and enable geofencing features
- **Usage**: Location data is used for core app functionality including photo location recognition, nearby place discovery, and location-based alerts
- **Storage**: Location data is stored locally on your device and optionally in our secure servers if you choose to save locations

### Photos and Images
- **Camera Access**: We access your camera to capture photos for location recognition
- **Photo Library**: We access your photo library to read EXIF metadata and GPS coordinates
- **Processing**: Photos are temporarily processed to extract location data and identify landmarks
- **Storage**: Photos are not permanently stored on our servers unless you explicitly save them to collections

### Device Information
- **Device ID**: We generate a unique device identifier for geofence notifications
- **Usage**: Device IDs ensure notifications are sent to the correct device
- **Storage**: Device IDs are stored locally and in our database

### User-Generated Content
- **Saved Locations**: Locations you save to collections
- **Collections**: Custom collections you create
- **Notes and Tags**: Any notes or tags you add to locations

## How We Use Your Information

- Provide core app functionality (location recognition, nearby places)
- Enable geofencing and location-based notifications
- Save and organize your location collections
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Provide customer support

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Share your location data with advertisers
- Use your photos for any purpose other than location recognition
- Share your data without your explicit consent

We MAY share data with:
- **Google Maps API**: For location services and mapping
- **Cloud Storage**: For backing up your saved locations (if enabled)
- **Analytics Services**: Anonymized usage data to improve the app

## Data Security

- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Location data is stored securely with industry-standard encryption
- Photos are processed temporarily and not stored permanently
- You can delete your data at any time

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Opt-out of location tracking
- Disable geofencing features
- Export your saved locations

## Data Retention

- **Location History**: Stored until you delete it
- **Photos**: Processed temporarily, not stored permanently
- **Collections**: Stored until you delete them
- **Device Data**: Stored while app is installed

## Children's Privacy

Pic2Nav is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Google Maps Platform**: For mapping and location services
- **Expo Services**: For app infrastructure
- **Cloud Storage**: For optional data backup

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Permissions Explained

### Required Permissions
- **CAMERA**: To capture photos for location recognition
- **ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION**: To get precise GPS coordinates
- **ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION**: To get approximate location
- **ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION**: For geofencing alerts when app is closed
- **READ_MEDIA_IMAGES**: To access photos from your library
- **ACCESS_MEDIA_LOCATION**: To read GPS data from photo EXIF metadata

### Optional Permissions
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS**: To send geofence alerts
- **WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE**: To save processed photos (Android 12 and below)
