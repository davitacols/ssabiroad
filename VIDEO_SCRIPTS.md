## Production Tips

### Lighting
- Film during golden hour (6-7 AM or 5-6 PM)
- Avoid harsh midday sun
- Use natural light when possible

### Audio
- Use phone mic (good enough)
- Minimize background noise
- Add trending audio in editing

### Editing
- Use CapCut (free)
- Add captions (80% watch without sound)
- Use transitions sparingly
- Keep it fast-paced

### Thumbnails
- Bright, high contrast
- Text overlay: "AI Found This!"
- Surprised face expression
- Before/after split screen
