# Privacy Policy for Pic2Nav

**Last Updated:** January 2025  
**Effective Date:** January 2025

## 1. Introduction

Pic2Nav ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Location Data (Required)
**What we collect:**
- Precise GPS coordinates from photo EXIF metadata
- Device location (when permission granted)
- Approximate location for nearby places

**Why we collect it:**
- Extract location data from photos (core functionality)
- Provide location-based features (nearby places, weather)
- Enable optional geofence alerts for saved locations
- Match photos to real-world coordinates

**How we collect it:**
- From photo metadata (EXIF data)
- From device GPS (when you grant permission)
- Only when you use location features

**Storage & Retention:**
- Saved locations stored locally on your device
- Location history stored locally (optional feature)
- No cloud backup of location data
- Deleted when you remove saved locations or uninstall app

### 2.2 Photos and Videos (Required)
**What we collect:**
- Photos you select or capture for analysis
- Photo metadata (EXIF data including GPS, camera settings, timestamp)

**Why we collect it:**
- Identify locations from photos (core functionality)
- Analyze building features and architectural elements
- Extract GPS coordinates from metadata
- Provide location identification service

**How we process it:**
- Photos processed temporarily for analysis
- Sent to our secure API for AI analysis
- NOT stored permanently on our servers
- Deleted immediately after processing

**Storage & Retention:**
- Processed ephemerally (temporarily)
- Analysis results cached for 30 days
- Original photos never stored on our servers
- You control photos on your device

### 2.3 Files and Documents (Required)
**What we collect:**
- EXIF metadata from photos
- GPS coordinates embedded in photos
- Camera settings and timestamps

**Why we collect it:**
- Extract location data for identification
- Provide EXIF editing tools
- Enable GPS geotagging features

**Storage & Retention:**
- Metadata processed in real-time
- Not stored separately from photos
- Deleted with photo analysis results

### 2.4 Device Information
**What we collect:**
- Device model and OS version
- App version and settings
- Error logs and crash reports

**Why we collect it:**
- Improve app performance
- Fix bugs and crashes
- Optimize for different devices

**Storage & Retention:**
- Stored anonymously
- Retained for 90 days
- Cannot identify individual users

### 2.5 Data We DO NOT Collect
- ❌ Personal identifiable information (name, email, phone)
- ❌ Device IDs or advertising IDs
- ❌ Contacts or messages
- ❌ Payment information
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Audio recordings

## 5. Foreground Service (Background Location)

**When it's used:**
- ONLY for optional geofence alerts feature
- You must explicitly enable this feature
- Not required for core functionality

**What it does:**
- Monitors proximity to your saved locations
- Sends notifications when entering/exiting geofence areas
- Tracks location history for journey mapping (optional)

**User control:**
- Persistent notification shown when active
- "Stop tracking" button in notification
- Can disable anytime in app settings
- Can revoke permission in device settings

**Data handling:**
- Location data stored locally only
- Not sent to servers
- Deleted when you disable feature

## 6. Data Storage and Security

### Storage Locations
- **Local device:** Saved locations, app settings, location history
- **Secure cloud:** Temporary photo analysis (deleted after processing)
- **No permanent cloud storage** of personal data

### Security Measures
- ✅ HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- ✅ Secure local storage (Expo SecureStore)
- ✅ No permanent server-side storage of photos
- ✅ Regular security audits
- ✅ Minimal data collection principle

### Data Retention
- **Photos:** Processed ephemerally, not stored
- **Analysis results:** Cached 30 days, then deleted
- **Saved locations:** Until you delete them
- **Location history:** Until you delete or disable feature
- **Error logs:** 90 days

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
- ✅ View all saved locations in app
- ✅ Delete individual locations
- ✅ Clear all location history
- ✅ Export your data (saved locations)

### Permission Management
- ✅ Revoke camera permission (Settings → Apps → Pic2Nav)
- ✅ Revoke location permission (Settings → Apps → Pic2Nav)
- ✅ Revoke photo access (Settings → Apps → Pic2Nav)
- ✅ Disable background location
- ✅ Disable geofence alerts in app

### Data Deletion
- ✅ Delete saved locations: App → Settings → Clear Data
- ✅ Delete location history: App → Settings → Clear History
- ✅ Request full data deletion: support@pic2nav.com
- ✅ Uninstall app removes all local data

### Opt-Out Options
- ✅ Don't enable geofence alerts (no background location)
- ✅ Don't save locations (no local storage)
- ✅ Use app without location permission (limited features)
