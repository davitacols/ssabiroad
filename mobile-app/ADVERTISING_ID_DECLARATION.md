# Advertising ID Declaration

## For Google Play Console

**Does your app use advertising ID?**

**Answer: NO**

Pic2Nav does not use advertising ID. The app does not display ads, does not track users for advertising purposes, and does not collect or transmit advertising identifiers.

## How to Declare in Play Console

1. Go to **App content** in Play Console
2. Find **Advertising ID** section
3. Select **No, my app doesn't use advertising ID**
4. Save

The app is ad-free and does not integrate any advertising SDKs or tracking libraries.
